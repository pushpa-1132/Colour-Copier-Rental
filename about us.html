About Rent Copier

Rent Copier was founded with the vision of providing affordable and flexible office copier rental solutions to businesses across Singapore. We understand that every office has unique printing and copying needs, and we strive to offer a range of high-quality copiers and multifunction printers that meet those requirements without the burden of large upfront investments.

With over 25 years in the industry, our dedicated team has developed deep knowledge and expertise in office equipment rental, maintenance, and support, making Rent Copier a trusted partner to startups, SMEs, and large enterprises alike.

Our extensive inventory features trusted brands including FujiFilm, Canon, Epson, and Ricoh, ensuring our clients receive reliable, efficient, and cutting-edge technology. We offer personalized rental plans with dedicated technical support, allowing your business operations to run smoothly without disruption.

At Rent Copier, we are committed not only to delivering dependable office solutions but also to delivering exceptional customer service. Our mission is to simplify your office equipment needs with seamless rental experiences, expert maintenance, and prompt emergency response services.

Beyond rental, we support our clients with integrated office services such as copier disposal, relocation, and IT product rentals. Our vision is to empower businesses to focus on growth and success while we take care of their copier and technology requirements. When you choose Rent Copier, you choose reliability, professionalism, and partnership.
